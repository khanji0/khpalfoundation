Our Mission

At Khpal Foundation, our mission is to ensure no one goes to bed hungry.

We are dedicated to providing meals to those in need and fostering a sense of community where everyone supports one another. Through compassion, unity, and collective action, we strive to bring hope, nourishment, and dignity to every individual we serve.

Our work is built on three core values:

  • Compassion We care deeply about every person we serve
  • Unity We believe in the power of community coming together
  • Dignity We treat everyone with respect and honor

Our Story

Khpal Foundation is a campaign born from my deep commitment to fighting hunger in Pakistan. This initiative is not just a project, it's a dream I've had for many years. I've always tried my best to help those in need, but now I need your support to take this cause to the next level.

With rising inflation and unemployment, the hunger crisis in Pakistan is worse than ever. I've personally seen the devastating effects of hunger on vulnerable people, disabled individuals, elderly men and women, who find themselves without work and unable to afford even a single meal a day. I've witnessed young children on the streets, desperately in need of food and care. I've seen elderly men with white beards, their eyes filled with tears, as they worry about how they will feed their families.

This heartbreaking reality has deeply moved me and fueled my determination to make a meaningful change. Khpal Foundation is my response to the hunger crisis in Pakistan, a foundation created to provide food, support, and hope to those in need.

Together, we can make a difference.

The name "Khpal" means "self" in Pashtu, and I chose this name because it reflects the spirit of selflessness and care for others. This foundation is for all those who feel the same way and want to help.

Help us support this mission.

Jibran Ahmed Khan

Founder & Director
